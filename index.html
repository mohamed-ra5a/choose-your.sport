Why sports matter

Physical activity helps build fitness, supports brain health, improves mood, and strengthens the heart, muscles, and bones. Teens should aim for at least 60 minutes of moderate-to-vigorous activity each day, with muscle- and bone-strengthening activities included.

Better health

Regular activity supports healthy growth and long-term well-being.

More energy

Moving your body can help you feel more active and focused.

Stronger habits

Choosing a sport you enjoy makes it easier to stay consistent.
