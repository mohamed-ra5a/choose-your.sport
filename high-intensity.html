Best choices for this goal

These sports are popular because they keep the heart rate up and use many muscles.

Swimming

Full-body movement that is great for endurance and overall fitness.

Jump Rope

Simple, cheap, and excellent for coordination and cardio.

Running

Easy to start and perfect for improving stamina.

Cycling

Good for longer sessions and building leg endurance.

What to consider

Enjoyment matters

The best sport is the one you can keep doing regularly.

Start safely

Begin at a comfortable level and increase the time gradually.

Stay consistent

Short sessions done often are better than quitting too early.
